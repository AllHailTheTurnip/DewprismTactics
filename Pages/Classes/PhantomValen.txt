Title: Phantom Valen|
Subtitle: Immortal Ghost|
Description: In response to the death of Idealism at the turn of the century, a secret society of scholars formed under
the singular mission objective: to survive the end of the world and create a new one rooted in reason and rationality.
The gardeners of this new world would be called Phantom Valens: men who had forsaken their mortal forms to remain forever
in the mortal realm as powerful spirits.
    <p>Though the end of the world never came, the Phantom Valens persist. Solemn yet not solitary, they live under
    a message of rebirth without redemption, carrying an idealistic vision which much like themselves refuses to die.</p>
    <p>Phantom Valens specialize is alteration magic, using their vast arsenal of spells to twist and warp reality
    as they see fit. They can weaken their enemies, sabotage strategies, and strengthen their allies with impunity.</p>|
